Proposal

- Existing System:

The problems faced in the existing system are that people have to visit the hospital at least once to check the doctor’s availability or confirm over phone call whether the doctor is available or not. For the appointments, yet once again they have to visit the hospitals and wait in long queues before they reach the counter and then pay for doctor’s appointment. And then on top of it, they have to wait for their turns before they can see their medical practitioner.

- Proposed Solution:

A website comprising of an online doctor appointment booking system so that people make online bookings which will be hassle-free and save their time which they have to spend waiting in long queues before making appointments and before getting to meet the doctor they have taken appointment for.

- Main Idea:

The Online Doctor Appointment Booking System is a simple project developed using PHP, JavaScript, and CSS. The project consists of an admin and a user. The role of the admin is an important one that is managing schedules, patient records, appointment time, treatment facilities etc. The user will visit the website and register for appointment. The appointments for each doctor will be available on the day the doctor will be consulting their patients.
